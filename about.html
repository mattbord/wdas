We are a Australian made and based company that are striving to create a meal planner that can be used across the planet. We also trying to make the world a better place by putting some health back into it!

Global food wastage is a major problem across the globe so here at Fridge 2 Plate we want to eliminate this by making sure all your food is used and in a healthy way!

By utilising our app, you will be able to select the ingredients that are in your fridge and we will give you hundreds of recipes to chose from! Just select the ones you want to use and away you go!
